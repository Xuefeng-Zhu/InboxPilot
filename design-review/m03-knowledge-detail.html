Refund policy v2

Manual · last updated 2h ago by Maya Chen · 14 chunks · Ready

Overview

This document is the canonical refund policy for all paid plans on InboxPilot. It supersedes refund policy v1 (2024-09-12) and is the single source of truth for AI drafts and human replies.

Eligibility

Refunds are available within 30 days of the original charge, regardless of plan tier. After 30 days, refunds require approval from the payments team and are evaluated case-by-case.

Processing time

Approved refunds typically appear on the customer's statement within 3–5 business days, though some banks may take up to 10 business days. If a refund has not appeared after 7 business days, escalate to the payments team.

How to issue

Use the payments dashboard → search by email → "Refund" action. The system will issue a Stripe refund and trigger a confirmation email within 60 seconds.
