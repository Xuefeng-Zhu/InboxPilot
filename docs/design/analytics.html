Frame 02 · KPI tile anatomy

Six tiles, same shape, different meaning

Every tile is the same five-row block: label, value, delta, sparkline. Two visual states are reserved for the boundaries — ochre border on watch, terra border on action. Inverse metrics (escalations, response time when it's already fast) flip the delta polarity.

Page-level status pill rule

The pill is the worst of all six tiles — never averages, never rounds up.
Healthy
All 6 tiles green. Pill is forest.
Watch
At least one tile yellow, none red. Pill is ochre with a one-line reason.
Action
At least one tile red. Pill is terra, reason is the worst-offending metric.

A support lead can show this to a stakeholder in 5 seconds. Green is left, red is right; the marker says where we are.

Frame 06 · mobile · 390px

One column, full story, no scroll fatigue
